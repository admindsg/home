02 · The Opportunity Gap

The problem is not talent. It is opportunity.

Cost, confidence gaps, disconnected pathways, and gatekeeping can keep music hidden. DSG builds warm, structured entry points so people can learn, make sound, and move toward public proof.

03 · The Garden Map

Seed → Roots → Stem → Bloom → Garden

A guided pathway connects curiosity to musicianship, recording support, performance proof, and community growth.

  1. SeedTalent, curiosity, hidden music
  2. RootsMusicianship, confidence, learning
  3. StemRecording opportunity, structure, support
  4. BloomPerformance, songs, public proof
  5. GardenCommunity, donors, partners, shared growth
