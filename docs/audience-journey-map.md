# Discovery Sound Garden Audience Journey Map

| Audience | Emotional state | Question in their mind | Homepage section that answers it | Friction point | Best CTA | What success looks like |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Curious beginner | Nervous, unsure, curious | “Is there a place where I can begin without feeling judged?” | Hero / threshold and Find Your Starting Point | Fear of being behind or not musical enough | Find Your Path | They choose the learning path and feel safe taking a first step. |
| Returning adult musician | Hopeful, rusty, cautious | “I used to do this. Can I start again?” | Find Your Starting Point and Garden Map | Shame about time away from music; unclear structure | Start Learning | They understand DSG offers a warm, structured way back into musicianship. |
| Emerging artist/songwriter | Motivated, idea-filled, under-supported | “Can someone help me turn ideas into real songs or demos?” | Garden Map and What Grows Here | Cost, lack of recording support, disconnected next steps | Build Your Sound | They see a pathway from learning to recording to public proof. |
| Donor | Values-driven, careful, proof-seeking | “Will my gift create a concrete opportunity?” | Sponsor Opportunity and Trust / Footer | Need for 501(c)(3) clarity and specific impact | Sponsor Opportunity | They understand gift levels and donate through Zeffy. |
| Partner/community organization | Practical, mission-aligned, planning-oriented | “Can DSG support our community with credible programming?” | What Grows Here, Garden Map, and Trust / Footer | Needs program clarity and contact route | Partner With DSG | They identify a program fit and reach out to discuss partnership. |
