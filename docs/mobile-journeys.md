# Mobile and Cross-Device Journey Maps

## 1. Curious beginner on phone

- **Actor:** First-time adult or student interested in music.
- **Scenario:** Opens DSG from Instagram or a shared link.
- **Expectation:** Quickly learn whether DSG welcomes beginners and what to do next.

| Phase | Actions | Mindset | Emotion | Friction | Opportunity | Homepage implication | CTA sequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival | Reads hero and tagline | “What is this?” | Curious | Too much abstract copy can lose them | State NYC nonprofit + learn/record/perform immediately | Short hero with Find Your Path | Find Your Path |
| Self-identify | Taps beginner path | “Am I allowed to start?” | Nervous | Music can feel expert-only | Reassure that Foundations/workshops are entry points | Path panel for beginners | Start with Foundations |
| Evaluate | Scans program details | “What will I learn?” | Hopeful | Dates/cost unclear | Show beginner outcomes and next availability | Program accordion | Get Event Notifications |
| Act | Opens notification form | “Tell me when I can join.” | Motivated | Embed may fail | Fallback link to Zeffy notification form | Lazy iframe + fallback | Open notification form |

- **Actor:** Singer interested in Vanguard Voices.
- **Scenario:** Sees a rehearsal/performance post or hears about the chorus.
- **Expectation:** Understand the vibe, standards, and join path.

| Phase | Actions | Mindset | Emotion | Friction | Opportunity | Homepage implication | CTA sequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival | Scans hero | “Is this a chorus?” | Interested | DSG includes more than singing | Mention performance pathway in hero/subhero | Hero plus path selector | Find Your Path |
| Select path | Chooses “I want to sing with others” | “Is Vanguard Voices for me?” | Excited/anxious | Audition anxiety | Explain care + standards | Singer path panel | Explore Vanguard Voices |
| Proof | Views Vanguard Voices module | “Can I picture myself there?” | Encouraged | No real photo/video can weaken proof | Use approved rehearsal/performance image/video when available | Proof module with fallback | Get Vanguard Voices Updates |

- **Actor:** Artist with songs/ideas but limited recording resources.
- **Scenario:** Visits after hearing DSG offers mobile recording opportunity.
- **Expectation:** Determine whether DSG can help create demos, content, or performance proof.

| Phase | Actions | Mindset | Emotion | Friction | Opportunity | Homepage implication | CTA sequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival | Reads opportunity-gap section | “They get the problem.” | Seen | Generic nonprofit copy would lose credibility | Keep cost/gatekeeping language | Opportunity explainer | Find Your Path |

| Phase | Actions | Mindset | Emotion | Friction | Opportunity | Homepage implication | CTA sequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## 5. Partner on desktop or tablet

- **Actor:** School, nonprofit, community org, or arts partner.
- **Scenario:** Evaluates DSG for a workshop, residency, recording activation, or showcase.
- **Expectation:** Find credibility, fit, services, and inquiry path.

| Phase | Actions | Mindset | Emotion | Friction | Opportunity | Homepage implication | CTA sequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival | Reads hero and mission | “Are they credible?” | Analytical | Too much metaphor can reduce confidence | Lead with nonprofit music pathway | Hero with concise identity | Partner with DSG |
| Explore | Reviews programs | “What can they deliver?” | Practical | Program offerings may be vague | Compare workshops/residencies/recording/showcases | Program accordion/comparison | Start a Partner Conversation |
| Validate | Looks for proof | “Have they produced outcomes?” | Cautiously optimistic | Missing impact examples | Use Vanguard Voices and verified partner proof | Proof module | Start a Partner Conversation |
| Inquire | Opens partner panel | “Can I start a conversation?” | Ready | No form/contact clarity | Provide selected-interest mailto/form fallback | Partner inquiry panel | Send partner inquiry |
