# Discovery Sound Garden image sourcing brief

Use this brief when replacing placeholder Cloudflare Images URLs. Do not hotlink stock images. Download according to license, upload to Cloudflare Images, and record the final metadata in `assets/js/site-data.js`.

## Hero
- Emotional purpose: Immediately communicate possibility, warmth, and credibility.
- Show: Real DSG music moment, warm rehearsal, instrument detail, or human creative support.
- Avoid: Fake class photos, fake AI participants, elite conservatory imagery.
- Search terms: warm music rehearsal, community music workshop, piano notebook hands, microphone rehearsal room.
- Real DSG required: Strongly preferred before launch.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable only as abstract music/garden or non-identifying detail.
- Alt direction: Describe the real activity and DSG context.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm participant consent and photographer/license approval.

## Start Learning
- Emotional purpose: Reduce intimidation.
- Show: Sheet music, keyboard, notebook, hands learning music, beginner-friendly setting.
- Avoid: Advanced performance imagery that feels too elite.
- Search terms: beginner piano notebook, sheet music pencil, music lesson hands.
- Real DSG required: Preferred, not required for first launch.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable for music materials or instrument details.
- Alt direction: Emphasize beginner-friendly music learning.
- Consent/licensing: Consent needed if identifiable people appear.

## Vanguard Voices / Sing With Others
- Emotional purpose: Show belonging, voice, and community.
- Show: Real choir/community singing or rehearsal if available.
- Avoid: Stock choir that looks too formal or unrelated.
- Search terms: community choir rehearsal, singers music stands, microphone rehearsal room.
- Real DSG required: Preferred for credibility.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable only for microphone, stands, room, or abstract voice visuals.
- Alt direction: Describe singing together and community rehearsal.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm consent for every identifiable singer.

## Create or Record
- Emotional purpose: Show that ideas can become real work.
- Show: Mic, laptop, notebook, songwriting session, recording setup.
- Avoid: Luxury studio imagery that makes DSG feel expensive or out of reach.
- Search terms: home recording microphone notebook, songwriter laptop microphone, recording interface hands.
- Real DSG required: Not required, but useful.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable for non-identifying equipment details.
- Alt direction: Describe tools for recording or songwriting.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm license and avoid visible private screen information.

## Partner Programming
- Emotional purpose: Signal that schools and organizations can work with DSG.
- Show: Community space, teaching moment, group learning, instruments in a circle.
- Avoid: Corporate handshake stock photo.
- Search terms: community music workshop setup, music classroom instruments circle, school music workshop.
- Real DSG required: Preferred for partner credibility.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable for empty room or workshop setup.
- Alt direction: Describe a prepared community music programming space.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm site and participant permissions.

## Donation Impact
- Emotional purpose: Make giving feel connected to opportunity.
- Show: Human/community moment or symbolic pathway/growth image.
- Avoid: Sad charity imagery or generic donation stock photos.
- Search terms: music materials community, instrument hands warm, garden pathway music.
- Real DSG required: Not required.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable for symbolic pathway/growth or music materials.
- Alt direction: Connect music materials to sponsored opportunity.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm license and consent if people appear.

## Founder / Trust
- Emotional purpose: Build trust and reduce uncertainty.
- Show: Real founder/instructor photo.
- Avoid: AI portraits or generic teacher stock photos.
- Search terms: Not applicable; commission or select real photo.
- Real DSG required: Yes before launch if using a person image.
- Temporary stock/AI: Use only a text/initials placeholder.
- Alt direction: Name and role of the founder/instructor.
- Consent/licensing: Founder approval and photographer permission required.

## Newsletter / Event Notifications
- Emotional purpose: Make joining updates feel low-pressure.
- Show: Simple music/garden graphic or warm abstract visual.
- Avoid: Busy collage.
- Search terms: music garden abstract, sound wave botanical, warm notebook music.
- Real DSG required: No.
- Temporary stock/AI: Acceptable as abstract support visual.
- Alt direction: Describe the graphic, not a fake event.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm license.

## Open Graph
- Emotional purpose: Make shared links look professional.
- Show: Simple branded image with the exact tagline.
- Avoid: Text-heavy image that becomes unreadable on mobile.
- Search terms: Not applicable; create brand graphic.
- Real DSG required: No.
- Temporary stock/AI: Abstract support is acceptable.
- Alt direction: Branded DSG social share image with tagline.
- Consent/licensing: Confirm rights to any background image or artwork.
